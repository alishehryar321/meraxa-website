About Meraxa

Meraxa International is a premium manufacturer and exporter of home textiles and apparel for the global B2B market. We combine heritage craftsmanship with modern sustainability practices to serve clients in Europe, the Middle East, and beyond.

All of our products are produced under certified ethical standards and quality control frameworks. We tailor bulk orders, custom labels, and packaging to your retail and wholesale needs.
